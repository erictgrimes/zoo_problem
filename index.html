Giraffes

  1. Frankie

Giraffes are the tallest land animals, distinguished by their long necks and legs, unique spotted coats, and long, prehensile tongues that help them reach high foliage.

Lions

  1. Mella

Lions are large, powerful cats, apex predators with muscular bodies, short, rounded heads, and round ears, known for their golden-yellow fur and the prominent manes of adult males.
